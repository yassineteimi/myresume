Replatforming AI software on RedHat Openshift

Description

  • Major Bank in France (Confidential)

  • 02 december, 2022

X-Bank is a French bank that offers retail banking, insurance, private banking, and financial services for businesses. The bank has deployed artificial intelligence (AI) technologies on a large scale to serve its customers and assist its employees, their trajectory towards AI started about six years ago, initiating a reflection in partnership with IBM. In consequence, the bank created a specific department dedicated to AI, which is responsible for continuous innovation, implementing an industrialized organization, and deploying use cases. The main project goal is to provide this department with the right tools helping to face the numerous challenges of deploying AI at scale in production, first of all, by delivering a GA product release based on Openshift, taking into consideration the infrastructure, security, and technical stack constraints. Regarding the functional use case, we want to demonstrate to the client that he can use the AI's governance software in a fraud detection use case.

Business impact

  • Advancing a 200k euros deal
  • The project contributes to the business expansion of a 36M euros large deal.
  • Helping the bank justify the AI use to regulatory bodies and prevent costly fines.
  • Take informed and unbiased decisions when it comes to integrate AI in critical processes.
  • Objectively assess the relevance of AI and the associated expected gains.

Technologies
