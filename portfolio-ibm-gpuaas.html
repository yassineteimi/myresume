GPUaaS software on RedHat Openshift

Description

  • Credit Agricole

  • March 2022 to June 2022

Credit agricole IT department wants to manage their GPU resources efficiently, and have a visual dashboard to monitor their usage in real time. This project's goal is to provide a GPU "as a service" scheduling tool, to help their data science teams, plan their machine/deeplearning workloads, empowering inference and serve all data science teams efficiently, with performance and cost optimization
