infrastructure and applicaton performance monitoring with Instana

Description

  • CYPATH – French Healthcare Company

  • From March 2024 to June 2024

CYPATH’s core business application, used remotely by all its laboratories, suffered from frequent performance slowdowns. However, the CTO and IT team lacked the tools to diagnose and address the root cause of these issues effectively.
The goal was to implement a Proof of Concept (PoC) with Instana to gain deeper visibility into the application’s performance, identify bottlenecks, and resolve performance degradation.

This initiative involved collaboration with the third-party software vendor and aimed at creating a seamless integration for real-time monitoring and diagnostics.

Main responsibilities

  • Led design thinking workshops and technical sessions to outline the PoC scope, define performance degradation scenarios, and customize dashboards tailored to the client’s needs.
  • Spearheaded the installation and configuration of Instana within CYPATH’s on-premises environment, ensuring compliance with the client’s IT architecture.
  • Managed the entire PoC, overcoming technical challenges and providing architecture guidance to both the client and partner teams.
  • Delivered technical presentations and product demos using Instana SaaS, showcasing advanced observability capabilities that bridged the gap between infrastructure and application performance monitoring.
  • Collaborated with the third-party system integrator to streamline the PoC implementation and gain insights into the application’s architecture for optimal observability.
  • Sized the observability environment, feeding critical data to the sales team to assist in crafting the commercial proposal.
  • Ensured full software compatibility and integration between the client’s applications and Instana’s observability tools.
  • Led partner enablement initiatives by mentoring Tionis personnel on Instana’s configuration, architecture design, and deployment.

Key achievements

  • Significantly improved the client’s ability to diagnose and resolve performance issues, reducing response times to technical incidents and improving the overall reliability of a critical business application.
  • Provided business and technical stakeholders with real-time visibility across their infrastructure and applications, transforming how the client approached incident management.
  • Played a pivotal role in integrating Instana’s observability with CYPATH’s core systems, enhancing system resilience and customer satisfaction.
  • Successfully bridged the gap between the client’s legacy on-premise systems and modern cloud-native solutions through comprehensive observability.
  • Enabled the sales team to deliver an accurate and well-positioned sales proposal based on real-world data from the PoC.
