As a Technical Leader and Odoo expert, my objective is to provide exceptional expertise and guidance to organizations seeking to optimize their business processes through the development, integration, and deployment of customized Odoo solutions. I specialize in driving successful projects that align with business objectives and enhance competitiveness. With a focus on the Cercle des Voyages which is a French luxury travel company based in Paris, I led the implementation of a tailored CRM solution on a private cloud, catering to the unique needs of their luxury travel clientele. This solution not only streamlines back-office operations but also offers an advanced and autonomous travel booking experience for customers, all within the unified Odoo platform.

Main responsibilities

  • Collaborated closely with client business owner and IT Director to understand their specific business requirements and challenges.
  • Leveraged my expertise in Odoo to design, develop, integrate, and deploy a custom CRM solution on a private cloud infrastructure.
  • Orchestrated the project as the tech lead, ensuring seamless coordination among the development team, stakeholders, and third-party vendors.
  • Implemented agile methodologies to prioritize and deliver key functionalities in iterations, ensuring continuous feedback and improvement.
  • Successfully configured and customized Odoo modules to meet the unique needs of Cercle des Voyages, such as streamlining travel document preparation, optimizing back-office workflows, and enhancing customer interactions through VOIP and SMS integration.
  • Collaborated with the UX/UI team to create an intuitive and user-friendly interface, enabling efficient interactions for back-office agents during travel document preparation and providing an advanced self-booking experience for customers.
  • Designed and implemented an integrated payment system, allowing customers to easily personalize their travel itineraries online, sign quotes electronically, make secure payments, and access additional features.
  • Proactively monitored the solution's performance, scalability, and security, implementing necessary optimizations and enhancements as required.
  • Actively engaged with stakeholders to provide project updates, address concerns, and align the solution with evolving business needs.

    The tailored CRM solution has streamlined and automated various back-office operations, enabling Cercle des Voyages to optimize their workflow efficiency. This enhanced operational efficiency has led to time savings, reduced errors, and improved overall productivity.

    The advanced self-booking functionality, personalized services, and seamless interactions provided by the Odoo CRM solution have significantly improved customer satisfaction.
