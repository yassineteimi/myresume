Embeded applications for taxi drivers

Description

  • G7 Taxis

  • November 2019, August 2020

I started this mission while G7 was in the midst of an important reorganisation, in fact, G7 was facing stiff competition from VTCs especially Uber in large public customers' segment, in consequence, G7 studied the situation, and came out with the conclusion that Uber are definetly winning in transactions' volume, but not necessarily with the service quality that a customer would expect.

In consequence, the business strategy turned to ensure high-quality service, and target professional customers (b2b) as their first priority. nevertheless, this strategy would never work without drivers, with that in mind, G7 aims to satisfy drivers and win their loyalty, considering them as a first-level customers.

In this perspective, my mission is to take the ownership of all solutions that help the driver in his day-to-day transportation activities, mainly mobile/tablet applications embeded with vehicle's hardware such as taximeter for example. The tablet application was already in production, and my responsibility was to take the solution ownership to develop and deploy new features requested by drivers, on the other hand, I designed from scratch the mobile application architecture, this app will be the driver companion when he's out of his vehicle, and will serve him to get notified about a customer's ride request in the area where the driver's vehicle is located.
