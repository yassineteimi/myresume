CRM integrated with website and e-commerce application

Description

  • UGC (France/Belgium)

  • www.ugc.fr

  • March 2017 to November 2017

To provide an optimized digital experience for UGC, a leading operational and logistics management company for cinemas in France and Belgium, the project aimed to implement an integrated CRM solution that caters to both their B2B and B2C operations. The primary goal was to deliver a comprehensive solution that seamlessly handles operational management, logistics, ticket reservations, ticket sales, membership programs, and promotional activities. As a Tech leader and Odoo expert, I took the leadership role to ensure the convergence of different teams, resulting in the development of the most optimal and user-friendly solution for UGC across both the backend and frontend domains.

Main responsibilities

  • Led a cross-functional team of developers, designers, and business analysts to implement an integrated CRM solution for UGC.
  • Continuously monitored the performance and stability of the implemented solution, proactively identifying areas for optimization and enhancement.
  • Presented project updates and milestones to senior management and stakeholders, ensuring transparent communication throughout the project.
  • Collaborated closely with stakeholders, including both internal teams and external partners, to understand business requirements and define the project scope.
  • Conducted thorough architectural assessments to identify the most suitable technologies and frameworks for the CRM implementation.
  • Designed a scalable and secure system architecture, ensuring seamless integration between the e-commerce platform for customers and the professional space for partners.
  • Utilized my expertise in CRM solutions to provide technical guidance and mentorship to the development team throughout the project lifecycle.
  • Implemented best practices and quality control measures to ensure the delivery of a robust and user-friendly solution.
  • Worked closely with the testing team to conduct comprehensive testing and resolve any issues or bugs that arose during the development process.
  • Successfully delivered the CRM solution within the agreed timeline and budget, meeting UGC's expectations.
