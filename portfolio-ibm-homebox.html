Description

  • HomeBox (a G7 company)

  • From March 2024 to June 2024

Instana End-to-End IT Observability (Infrastructure, Cloud & On-Prem Applications)

Homebox aimed to modernize its IT landscape through enhanced monitoring and observability as part of a broader cloud-native strategy. The project involved setting up Instana observability for both the legacy monolithic application “BoxPro Web” and the new microservices-based platform “Ariane” on OpenShift. The challenge was to ensure seamless coexistence of both systems, providing full observability for infrastructure and applications in cloud and on-premises environments, and delivering real-time insights to system administrators, software engineers, and network administrators. This project was executed in collaboration with a partner, Tionis, for which I mentored and trained their personnel on Instana’s installation, configuration, and architecture best practices.

Main responsibilities

  • Led the installation and configuration of Instana in both cloud and on-prem environments, adhering to data privacy and isolation principles.
  • Provided mentorship and training to Tionis personnel, empowering them to handle Instana’s architecture design and implementation.
  • Collaborated closely with Homebox’s system administrators, IT director, and Russian consultants to tailor the monitoring solution to their unique IT landscape.
  • Took strategic architecture decisions, balancing technical constraints and business requirements to deliver an optimized observability solution.
  • Utilized Kanban dashboards (Jira) to drive the Proof of Concept (PoC) efforts, ensuring on-time and efficient execution.
  • Delivered technical presentations and product demos, showcasing Instana’s capabilities and ensuring buy-in from key client stakeholders.
  • Collaborated with sales teams to create accurate observability environment sizing, supporting sales proposal development.
  • Engaged with the Instana product team to report bugs and enhance the official product documentation, ensuring future clients could benefit from improved user guides.
  • Created comprehensive PoC documentation, ensuring post-sales teams had a clear understanding of the architecture and implementation details.

Key achievements

  • Reduced Incident Response Time: Implemented Instana’s end-to-end observability, reducing incident response times by providing actionable insights across applications and infrastructure, enabling DevOps teams and SREs to diagnose issues faster.
  • Enhanced Application Performance: Improved observability on both legacy and microservices architectures, allowing for smoother transition during the migration process, and optimizing the performance of Homebox’s systems.
  • Improved Technical Knowledge Sharing: Successfully trained and mentored Tionis’ personnel, fostering a strong partner collaboration that empowered Tionis to independently manage Instana implementations.
  • Business Alignment: Worked closely with business stakeholders to ensure the observability solution aligned with Homebox’s digital transformation goals, driving better software quality and quicker time-to-market for their new services.

Technologies

  • Instana SaaS: For comprehensive application and infrastructure observability.
  • OpenShift: Container orchestration platform for the new “Ariane” microservices application.
  • Istio: Service mesh used for managing microservices traffic.
  • RabbitMQ: For message brokering between applications.
  • Azure SQL Server (RTM): For cloud-based database management.
  • .NET Framework 4.8 & .NET Core: Core backend technologies for BoxPro Web and Ariane.
  • IIS: Web server for hosting the monolithic application.
  • IBM Licensing management
  • DB2 on IBM i: Database for legacy systems.
