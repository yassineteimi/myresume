Instana End-to-End IT Observability (Infrastructure & On-Prem Applications)

Description

  • IRCEM (HealthCare insurance company)

  • From March 2024 to June 2024

IRCEM had existing monitoring tools for their on-premise infrastructure but lacked comprehensive observability covering their applications.
This gap impacted their ability to resolve technical incidents effectively, affecting the availability of their business-critical customer portal.
The portal is essential for maintaining IRCEM’s brand image and serves as a key interface with their customers.

Main responsibilities

  • Led design thinking and technical workshops to define the precise PoC perimeter and develop use case scenarios.
  • Directed Instana installation and configuration across IRCEM’s on-prem infrastructure, ensuring full compatibility with their IT landscape.
  • Delivered PoC implementation, overcoming technical and organizational challenges, and demonstrated critical functionalities in real-world scenarios.
  • Conducted technical presentations and led product increment demos for key stakeholders using Instana SaaS.
  • Collaborated with DBMarlin to ensure seamless integration with Instana, delivering advanced database observability capabilities.
  • Defined the observability environment sizing, which was used by sales representatives to build accurate sales proposals.
  • Ensured the compatibility of IRCEM’s software versions with the Instana observability platform, preventing operational disruptions.
  • Played a key role in partner enablement, mentoring and training Tionis personnel on Instana’s architecture design and implementation.

Business impact

  • Successfully delivered an end-to-end observability solution, significantly reducing incident response times.
  • Enhanced the reliability and performance monitoring of IRCEM’s critical customer portal, improving user satisfaction and business operations.
  • Streamlined cross-functional collaboration between IRCEM’s internal teams, Tionis, and DBMarlin, driving alignment on project goals.
  • Empowered technical teams through hands-on enablement workshops, boosting internal capabilities for long-term success with Instana.
